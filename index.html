About Rock&Bob Cafe

Rock&Bob Cafe was founded in 2018 by two music enthusiasts who wanted to create a space where people could enjoy great coffee and food while immersed in rock culture.

Our cafe features regular live acoustic sessions, a curated playlist of rock classics, and a menu inspired by legendary musicians.

We source our coffee beans from sustainable farms and prepare all our desserts in-house daily to ensure the highest quality.
